## Interests/Experience
I study efficient learning at scale. I'm particularly interested in how we can get rid of the full-precision weights in quantization-aware training of neural networks. This enables us to reap the memory benefits of low-precision formats not just during inference but also during training. A more comprehensive list of topics that interest me includes: quantization of LLMs, second-order optimizers, activation compression, data selection, distillation, and speculative decoding. 

Previously, I've worked as a student programmer at Danske Bank on fraud detection systems, and as a junior trading analyst at Danske Commodities.
